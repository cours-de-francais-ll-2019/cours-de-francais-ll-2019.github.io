L’alphabet

Les lettres de l’alphabet

There are in the French language 26 letters in the alphabet. You probably know the romaji so you know all the letters but can you pronounce them?

Let’s practice together!

Les voyelles 母音[ぼいん]

There are 6 vowels in the French language :

  • A - a
  • I - i
  • U - u
  • E - e
  • O - o
  • Y - y

Les consonnes 子音[しいん]

All the other letters of the alphabet are consonnants :

b, c, d, f, g, h, j, k, l, m, n, p, q, r, s, t, v, w, x, (y), z

Les accents アクサン

Some letters are not exactly pronounced the same, this is represented by the use of accents.
